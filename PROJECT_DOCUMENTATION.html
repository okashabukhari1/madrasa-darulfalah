The Three Portals

Admin

Manages the entire system.

  • Students, Teachers, Courses
  • Admissions & Messages
  • Gallery & Library
  • Announcements & Events
  • Exams, Attendance, Staff
  • User Accounts & Settings

Student

Views personal info.

  • Dashboard & Profile
  • My Courses
  • Attendance & Exams
  • Study Materials
  • Announcements

Teacher

Manages student progress.

  • Dashboard & Courses
  • Daily Progress Logs
  • Mark Attendance
  • Create & Grade Exams
  • Announcements
