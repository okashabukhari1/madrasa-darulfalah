📖 Most Popular

Hifz-ul-Quran

Complete memorization of the 30 Juz of the Holy Quran with proper Tajweed and Makharij. Students receive individual attention and regular revision sessions.

⏱ Duration: 3–5 Years
👤 Teacher: Hafiz Usman
📅 Days: Mon–Sat
🕐 Timing: 7AM – 2PM
✓ Age 7–16 · Basic Reading Required
🎵 Beginner Friendly

Tajweed ul Quran

Master the rules of Quranic recitation from Makharij al-Huruf to advanced Qira'at. Suitable for all ages wanting to perfect their recitation.

⏱ Duration: 1–2 Years
👤 Teacher: Qari Abdul Rehman
📅 Days: Mon, Wed, Fri
🕐 Timing: 4PM – 6PM
✓ All Ages · Can Read Arabic Letters
🕌 Advanced

Alim Course (Dars-e-Nizami)

Comprehensive 8-year program covering Tafseer, Hadith, Fiqh, Usul al-Fiqh, Arabic literature, and all major Islamic sciences following the Dars-e-Nizami curriculum.

⏱ Duration: 8 Years
👤 Teacher: Mufti Abdur Rauf
📅 Days: Mon–Sat
🕐 Timing: 8AM – 3PM
✓ Age 12+ · Hafiz Preferred
🌙 Language

Arabic Language (Classical & Modern)

Learn Classical Arabic (Fusha) to directly understand the Quran and Islamic texts, plus Modern Standard Arabic for communication and further scholarship.

⏱ Duration: 2 Years
👤 Teacher: Ustaz Ibrahim Al-Misri
📅 Days: Sun, Tue, Thu
🕐 Timing: 5PM – 7PM
✓ Age 10+ · No Prerequisites
⭐ All Ages

Islamic Studies (Foundation)

Foundation course covering the pillars of Islam, Aqeedah, basic Fiqh, Seerah of the Prophet ﷺ, and Islamic ethics. Perfect for beginners and children.

⏱ Duration: 1 Year
👤 Teacher: Maulana Saeed
📅 Days: Sat & Sun
🕐 Timing: 10AM – 12PM
✓ All Ages · No Prerequisites
📜 Specialized

Tafseer Course

In-depth study of Quranic exegesis covering classical and contemporary tafseer works including Tafseer Ibn Katheer, Tafseer Jalalayn, and Maariful Quran.

⏱ Duration: 3 Years
👤 Teacher: Dr. Khalid Mahmood
📅 Days: Mon–Thu
🕐 Timing: 2PM – 4PM
✓ Alim Students · Arabic Proficiency
📝 Advanced

Hadith Sciences

Study of the six major Hadith collections (Kutub al-Sittah) with focus on chain authenticity, narrator evaluation (Rijal), and practical application in Fiqh.

⏱ Duration: 4 Years
👤 Teacher: Mufti Abdur Rauf
📅 Days: Mon–Sat
🕐 Timing: 9AM – 11AM
✓ Alim Year 4+ · Arabic Required
✏️ Children

Quran Nazira (Reading)

Beginner program teaching children how to read the Quran with proper pronunciation, covering Arabic alphabet through complete Quran reading fluency.

⏱ Duration: 1–2 Years
👤 Teacher: Ustaza Maryam
📅 Days: Mon–Fri
🕐 Timing: 3PM – 5PM
✓ Age 5–10 · No Prerequisites
